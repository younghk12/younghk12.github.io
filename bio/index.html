I am a digital product designer based in Portland, Oregon. I recently graduated from Whitman College majoring in Theory and Practice in Digital Design. The individually planned major combined new media art, computer science, and art history with a focus on modern art and architecture. This unorthodox approach allowed me to get the most out of my liberal arts education.

My goal is to design and construct coherent websites and digital media through a critical and holistic design approach, inspired by the liberal arts and driven by rules and consistency. I enjoy breaking down complexity, whether that be organizing large amounts of information in a layout or using visual storytelling to communicate difficult concepts. I want to collaborate closely with software developers (and others from all disciplines) to make thoughtful digital products.

In my free time I enjoy waking up early for premier league weekends, searching out fluffy conditions to snowboard, and eating strange foods.
