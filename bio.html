Graphic Design

I am a digital product designer based in Portland, Oregon. I recently graduated from Whitman College majoring in Theory and Practice in Digital Design. The individually planned major combined new media art, computer science, and art history with a focus on modern art and architecture. My design background is slightly unorthodox, however planning my own major allowed me to get the most out of my liberal arts education.

I am currently searching for roles in user experience design involving elements of information architecture and brand identity design. I enjoy breaking down complexity, whether that be organizing large amounts of information in a layout or using visual storytelling to communicate difficult concepts. I want to collaborate closely with software developers (and others from all disciplines) to make thoughtful digital products.

In my free time I enjoy waking up early for premier league weekends, searching out fluffy conditions to snowboard, and eating strange foods.
